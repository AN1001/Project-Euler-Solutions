
#### Nth fibonacci number is:

$$\lceil\frac{\phi^n}{\sqrt{5}}\\rfloor$$

#### Hence:

$$\frac{\phi^n}{\sqrt{5}}\ > 10^{999}$$

$$log(\frac{\phi^n}{\sqrt{5}}\) > 999$$

$$\log{\phi^n} - log{\sqrt{5}}\ > 999$$

$$n \times log{\phi} > 999 + log{\sqrt{5}}\$$

$$n > \frac{999 + log{\sqrt{5}}}{log{\phi}} $$

$$n > 4781.85927075$$
